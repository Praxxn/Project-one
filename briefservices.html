Services We Provide



Insect Control Services


Cockroach Control


How it's treated:

Use of gel baiting in kitchen cabinets and drawers.

Residual spray in hiding areas (behind appliances, drains, etc.).

Odorless and safe for children & pets.

1–3 visits depending on infestation.


Rodent (Rat & Mouse) Control


How it's treated:

Glue boards or mechanical traps placed near rodent activity.

Rodenticide blocks placed in bait stations (tamper-proof).

Sealing of entry points (proofing).

Regular monitoring and replacement of bait.


Mosquito Control



How it's treated:

Thermal fogging in outdoor areas to kill adult mosquitoes.

Larvicide spraying in stagnant water to stop breeding.

Indoor ULV (ultra-low volume) misting with safe chemicals.

Weekly/bi-weekly treatments in high-risk zones.


Ant Control



How it's treated:

Use of sweet bait gels carried back to the colony.

Residual spray on walls, baseboards, and cracks.

Identification of ant species (sugar vs protein-based bait).

Reapplication if needed after 1-2 weeks.




Spider Control



How it's treated:

Removal of webs and nests using long-reach tools.

Residual spray in corners, ceilings, behind furniture.

Window and door sealing if infestation is high.

Monthly treatments if recurring.


Bee / Wasp Removal



How it's treated:

Eco-friendly knockdown spray applied directly to nests.

Removal of hives in safe, controlled methods.

Performed during evening hours (when bees are less active).

Use of PPE kits and advanced equipment for safety.


Termite Control



How it's treated:

✅ Pre-Construction

Soil treatment with chlorpyrifos or fipronil-based chemicals.

Application under slabs, flooring, and wall junctions.


✅ Post-Construction


Drilling holes at wall-floor junctions and injecting anti-termite chemical.

Trenching around the house for soil treatment.

3–5 year warranty service packages.


Silverfish Control



How it's treated:

Targeted spray treatment in cupboards, bookshelves, bathrooms.

Use of borate dust or diatomaceous earth in cracks and crevices.

Monitor using sticky traps.

Ensuring low humidity levels.


Lizard Control



How it's treated:

Use of herbal repellent sprays that deter lizards.

Application in ceilings, behind tube lights, and wall corners.

Use of glue boards near windows and light sources.

Sealing cracks to stop their entry




House fly Control



How it's treated:

Surface spray with contact insecticide.

Use of UV light flycatcher traps.

Garbage sanitation and fly breeding source identification.

Weekly or bi-weekly maintenance visits.




Bed Bug Control



How it's treated:

Two-step treatment: Initial spray and follow-up after 14 days.

High-pressure sprayer with odorless chemicals on mattresses, sofas, corners.

Heat treatment if required.

Advising customer on washing and drying bedding at high heat.
